West Boulevard Corridor

Transportation Study

ARCH STUDIO
Coral Rembert | Nicole Surace
Professor Anderson

Location

The location of this study is Charlotte, NC in Mecklenburg County along the West Boulevard Corridor.

Area of Influence

Areas that the West Boulevard Corridor reaches.

Highways and Thoroughfares

Major traffic arteries surrounding the West Boulevard Corridor area.

LYNX Lightrail System and Railroads

LYNX BlueLine and Proposed SilverLine routes and stops along with Railroads and their Right of Ways.

Bike Routes and Greenways

Existing bike routes with signs and existing greenways.

CATS Bus System

Charlotte's bus system with routes and stops.

Transportation Accessibility

Assessing current transportation accessibility within the Area of Influence using different distance radiuses.

Greenway Entry Points and Vicinity

Assessing current accessibility to Greenway entry points and the distances to destinations along the Greenway.

Key Nodes Transect

Assessing key nodes along West Boulevard and identifying intersections of opportunities along the stems.

Bus Route Transect

Assessing the amount of time people are spending on public transportation to get to and from major destinations.
